About Us

Varanasi's Most Trusted Real Estate Developer

Dharohar Buildcon has been transforming the skyline of Varanasi with premium residential and commercial projects. Located at PWD Office Road, Varuna Bridge, Nadesar, we are committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake.

Our portfolio includes a diverse range of properties from luxury apartments to commercial complexes, each built with meticulous attention to detail and quality. We believe in creating spaces that not only meet but exceed our clients' expectations.
